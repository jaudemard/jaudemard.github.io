Hello :)

I'm a Master's student in Bioinformatics, currently working at the French National Institute of Numerical Sciences and Technology (Inria) Pléiade team as an intern, where I'm supervized by Clémence Frioux.

My research focuses on metabolic networks, discrete models of metabolism and cyanobacteria-centered community. Through my Master's thesis and my internship at Inria, I've developped great interest in modelling approaches and multi-omics integration.

I'm passionate about systems biology, a huge scrabble enthusiast (I can beat your grandma) and I sketch in my free time. A fun fact about me is that I watched Samurai Champloo 11 times, and I still love it.
